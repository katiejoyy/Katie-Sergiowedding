FAQs

What is the dress code?
The dress code for our wedding is smart casual attire. Shirts, polo shirts or cocktail dresses.

What should I do if I can't make it?
You will be missed! If you can not make it to the wedding, please let us know as soon as possible and RSVP " No I can not attend" so we can plan accordingly

What will the weather be like this time of year?
Hopefully very warm and sunny! But please consider an umbrella (this is still England).

Will the reception be indoors?
Yes, with a terrace area open until 10pm

What time should I arrive?
Please arrive at the venue for 5:30PM

How do I get to the venue?
The address is: 12/13 New Wharf Rd, London N1 9RT.
The nearest station is King's Cross and is a 10 minute walk from there.
For more information please follow the link to the venue's website for more information:

Are kids welcome?
Yes kids are welcome! Make sure to include them in the RSVP form.

Can I bring a date?
We have a strict guest list to stay on budget. Our wedding is strictly RSVP only. We will only able to accommodate those listed on your invitation.

Will food be served?
We will have a buffet style meal and finger food served at the venue.

Will there be an open bar?
Yes, there will be an open bar

Will there be any vegan / vegetarian or special diet options?
We will be providing vegan options and if you have any special dietary requiements please let us know when you fill out RSVP

Can I take and post pictures of the wedding on social media?
Yes you are welcome to take pictures and post them on soical media. However we will have a professional photgrapher at the venue all day.

Where should guests park?
The museum does not have a car park. The nearest car park is the car park of St. Pancras International Station. This is a central London location so parking is very limited. We advise using public transport or taxis.

When is the RSVP deadline?
Please fill out the form above for each individual invitee before 6th July 2022.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?
Yes. London Canal Museum is one of the most easily accessible in England for disabled visitors. All parts of the museum are accessible to people with a disability and there is plenty of interest to enjoy.

Where are you registered?
We're not accepting gifts, but if you're feeling generous we have a honey moon fund instead! This can be in the form of cash on the day or a bank transfer if you prefer (just message one of us for details!) Thank you in advance!
